***

#### ``opacity?`` : [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)

***

#### ``size?`` : [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)

The size of the particle.


***

#### ``shapes?`` : [Shape](../types/Shape)[]

The shape of the particle.

***

#### ``speed?`` : [Vector](../types/Vector)
