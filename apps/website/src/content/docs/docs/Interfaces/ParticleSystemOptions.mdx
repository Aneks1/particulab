---
title: 'ParticleSystemOptions'
---

```ts
export default interface ParticleSystemOptions
```

## Properties

#### ``canvasSize`` : [Vector](../types/Vector)

The resolution the canvas will be set to on load.

***

#### ``amount?`` : [number](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Number)

The amount of particles to be rendered.

***

#### ``colors?`` : (RGBA | HEX)[]

The array of possible colors a particle can take.

***

#### ``fadeIn?`` : [FadeOptions](./FadeOptions)

Options for a fade in transition.

***

#### ``fadeOut?`` : [FadeOptions](./FadeOptions)

Options for a fade out transtion.

***

#### ``life?`` : [Interval](../types/Interval.md)

The lifetime of a particle as a range of possible values.

***

#### ``opacity?`` : [Interval](../types/Interval.md)

The opacity of a particle as a range of possible values.

***

#### ``size?`` : [Interval](../types/Interval.md)

The size of a particle as a range of possible values.


***

#### ``shapes?`` : [Shape](../types/Shape)[]

An array of possible shapes a particle can take.

***

#### ``speed?`` : [VectorInterval](../types/VectorInterval)

A 2D vector where x speed and y speed are taken as a range of possible values.
